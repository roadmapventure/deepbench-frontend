## 2. CONTEXT

The AI Audit "By Pattern" section currently shows 10 industry-standard AI patterns (PAT-01–PAT-10). The PATTERN_CATALOG in `useAIActivity.js` has 10 entries. The header strip reads "Patterns Active: 8/10".

This session expands the catalog to 20 patterns (PAT-01–PAT-20) by adding PAT-11 through PAT-20. All new entries are `active: false` — they appear in the By Pattern section as "Not yet active" chips, and auto-populate the existing Platform Roadmap "AI Patterns" section (no layout changes needed there).

Two existing patterns need special rendering in PatternRow:
- **PAT-10 HITL** — does not produce LLM calls; its metrics are "Gates Triggered" + "Avg Response Time" (both `—` until TI-18 ships). Mark as `hitlSpecial: true`.
- **PAT-14 Parallelization** — partially implemented (Test Team TT-01/02). Mark as `partial: true`. Shows a 🔶 chip instead of "Not yet active".

Header stat changes from hardcoded `/10` to dynamic `/${PATTERN_CATALOG.length}`.
