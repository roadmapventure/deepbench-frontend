## 2. CONTEXT

Two UI improvements to the AI Audit panel (`AIActivityPanel.jsx`).

**AI-32 — By Pattern inactive collapse**
The By Pattern section currently renders all 20 pattern rows flat. The 10+ inactive patterns (showing "Not yet active" chip) create visual noise below the active ones. Group them into a collapsed card — identical in style to the "Not yet called · N services" collapse already used in the By Service section. HITL (`hitlSpecial: true`) and Parallelization (`partial: true`) are special-case rows with custom chips — they stay outside the collapse as individual rows.

**AI-33 — Platform Roadmap redesign**
The Platform Roadmap section (collapsed by default) currently shows two flat lists: a "Services" heading with Now/Next/Later tiers, then a "AI Patterns" heading with Next/Later tiers. This mixes already-built items into a roadmap view. Replace with two 2-column cards — Next and Later only. Each card: AI Patterns column (left) + DeepBench Services column (right). No "Now" section — everything in the roadmap is unbuilt.
