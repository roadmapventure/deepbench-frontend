## 2. CONTEXT

"Re-run All" and "Update Steps →" are not yet fully tested for production use and are being held on the roadmap. Both buttons should be visually disabled and grayed out so users cannot trigger them. They remain visible on the screen — they are not hidden — so users understand the capability exists on the roadmap.

No other buttons are affected. The per-step "Re-run" button (inside `StepRow`, line ~233) is a separate feature and is NOT in scope for this session.

## 6. DESIGN RULES

- `opacity: 0.45` is the standard disabled treatment in this codebase
- `cursor: 'not-allowed'` on the button itself (HTML `disabled` does not apply cursor on all browsers without explicit override)
- Do NOT hide the buttons — they remain visible as roadmap indicators
- Do NOT add tooltip or label text — scope is disable only
