## 2. CONTEXT

Post-QA fixes and additions from S-AI01b manual QA (2026-06-11):

**Item 1 — Bug fix (QA FAIL):**
The AiBadge tooltip in the "Chat With Agent" panel header clips behind the panel's border. Root cause: the outer `ChatPanel` div (DashboardScreen.jsx line 289) has `overflow: "hidden"`. This clips all absolutely positioned children regardless of z-index. The header div (line 291) is a direct child — its tooltip balloon is cut off at the panel edge. Fix: add `overflow: "visible"` to the header div only. The outer container's `overflow: "hidden"` must remain (panel scroll behavior).

**Items 9, 10, 13 — Label corrections:**
- "+ Add Courses" button (PersonnelScreen.jsx ~L913) — change label from `"Knowledge Training"` to `"RAG & Routing Training"`
